About the Artist

It has long been acknowledged that Milo Ocean is a creative force in modern music. His work appeals to the heart while testing the intellect, and he is renowned for his ability to combine complex melodies with intense emotional resonance. Nature, quiet, and the human condition serve as inspiration for Milo's music, which has a very personal yet universal quality. His dedication to stretching artistic frontiers is demonstrated by his most recent album, The Sound of Stillness. Through sound and stillness, this 2024 release from TD Records, published by TD Music Distribution (TDMusic), provides a calm yet powerful experience.

The Album

The album, which clocks in at 56:50, is a journey through emotions, textures,
and moods. Each tune, which was produced by a group of gifted collaborators,
demonstrates Milo Ocean's ability to blend various voices into a cohesive whole.
The album strikes a mix between sincere simplicity and complex compositions, featuring guest performances
by well-known jazz and neo-soul performers.
